AmeriCorp Program Technical Assistance


Website: http://www.virginiaselfadvocacy.org/

Overview

The Partnership provides support to the Virginia Office of Volunteerism and Community Service to increase the number of applications and the enrollment of people with disabilities in national and community service and volunteer opportunities in Virginia.

Major Activities

  • Evaluation of current AmeriCorps State Program efforts to recruit and enroll people with disabilities in national and community service and volunteer opportunities
  • Development of presentations and materials to heighten interest in and to explain national and community service options to people with disabilities
  • Technical assistance and training to Virginia's existing AmeriCorps State Programs on disability awareness; cultivation of diverse service corps; and the recruitment and enrollment of people with disabilities in national and community service activities.
