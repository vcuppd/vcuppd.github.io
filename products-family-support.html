Creating Collaborative IEPs (Revised Second Edition)

It's About Me!

Research shows that students who participate in development of IEP has own are more likely to speak for themselves throughout their lives. The It's About Me! Guide provides steps to help parents, educators and students to ensure that the student has to say and control of their IEP, education and future. Students use the help and guidance of an adult to work through six steps designed with user-friendly tools. A second section and appendices provides checklists, tools and resources to support the development of the IEP. (It's About Me! It will consist of 56 pages, including tools, reference and resources.)
