Family to Family Health Information & Education Center


Website: Family to Family Network

Overview

With funding from the Maternal Child Health Bureau and the Virginia Department of Health, the Partnership for People with Disabilities, in collaboration with Medical Home Plus (MHP) and Parent to Parent of Virginia (PTPofVA) operates Virginia's Family to Family Health Information and Education Center (called VA F2F).

Major Activities

  • Individual Advocacy (MHP): Through a website and toll free number (877-264-8366), parents of Children and Youth with Special Health Care Needs (CYSHCN) have access to information and resources to help them in making decisions about their children's health care needs, and in accessing community supports. With additional support from the Virginia Department of Health, Cultural Liaisons to the African American and Latino Community provide resources and support to parents of CYSHCN in these communities. For the African American community contact the Liaison at 804-827-0423. For the Latino Community, the Liaison can be reached at 804-827-1547.
  • Peer Advocacy (in collaboration with Parent to Parent of Virginina and the Partnershipâ€™s Center for Family Involvement): Parents of CYSHCN will receive one to one emotional and systems navigational support through a network of experienced and trained parents and cultural brokers.
  • Systems Advocacy (the Partnership): A cadre of parents of CYSHCN have been trained as parent leaders advocating to improve the health care system in Virginia.
