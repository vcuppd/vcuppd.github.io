Access to Justice

This twenty five minute film was designed to assist judges and other professionals within the criminal justice system about the challenges faced by people with disabilities who are victims of domestic violence or sexual assault. With an introduction by Governor Tim Kaine, the film features three women with disabilities who relay their stories of their struggle to end the cycle of violence.

Violence Against People with Disabilities: The Response of the Criminal Justice System

In a collaborative effort between the Partnership for People with Disabilities and the Virginia Sexual and Domestic Violence Action Alliance, this curriculum was developed to train professionals within the Criminal Justice System about people with Disabilities who are victims of domestic violence and sexual Assault. Topics of chapters include an introduction to domestic violence and sexual assault, information about different types of disabilities, the Americans with Disabilities Act, area resources, and copies of handouts and group exercises.
