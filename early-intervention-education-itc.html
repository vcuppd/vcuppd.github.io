Integrated Training Collaborative (ITC)


Website: http://www.infantva.org/
Website: http://www.veipd.org/main/index.html

Overview

This project coordinates Virginia's personnel development and training for early intervention providers who offer supports and services under Part C of the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) - early intervention services for infants and toddlers with developmental delays and disabilities and their families. The ITC shares a commitment to a coordinated and consistent plan of preservice and inservice training as part of a Comprehensive System of Personnel Development (CSPD), in order to ensure the provision of services by highly qualified personnel. The ITC brings together the experience and expertise of providers, family members, university faculty, Part C staff, and other dedicated individuals to help implement training opportunities and enhance educational networking on behalf of infants and toddlers with developmental delays or disabilities and their families. (Funded by the Virginia Department of Mental Health, Mental Retardation, and Substance Abuse Services.)

Major Activities

  • Collaborate with the Part C office to provide training and information to early intervention personnel that is based on current research, is consistent with Part C federal and Virginia regulations and reflects up-to-date effective practices, particularly related to Virginia's Individualized Part C Early Intervention Supports and Services in Everyday Routines, Activities and Places
  • Plan and coordinate the statewide early intervention conference
  • Coordinate and train service coordinators utilizing the Kaleidoscope: New Perspectives in Service Coordination, Level I and Level II curricula.
