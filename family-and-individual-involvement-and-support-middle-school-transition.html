Middle School Transition


Website: http://www.alife4me.com/

Overview

The Virginia Department of Education and the Partnership for People with Disabilities have collaborated to develop a free, interactive website for middle school students with more significant disabilities and their parents to engage in a process of self-identity and planning for the future. The website enables the middle school students, along with their parents and teachers, to identify strengths, challenges and interests, and their post-high school visions and to develop a plan for their middle school and high school years that will lead to a life they have identified for themselves.

The interactive and engaging website will include activities for developing a personal profile (Who Am I?) and leads the user to taking an animated tour of a community like their own and explore options about where people work/volunteer, live, eat, hang out and have fun. The personal characteristics and choices about their desires for their future will result in a personal "profile" that can be printed and shared with the student's IEP team.
