Donate

Please partner with us as we continue to serve people with disabilities and strive to build communities that serve everyone.
As our newest partner you can help us continue our work not only in Richmond but throughout Virginia.

I want to support the Partnership

Let us put your contribution to work where it is most needed
Your unrestricted gift allows us to channel your contribution where it is most needed at any point in time.

Memorials and gifts that honor
Remember or honor a family member or a friend with a special donation in their name. We will notify those whom you honor or remember.

Matching gifts
Many companies match employee contributions. Contact your Human Resources office for information on how to double your gift!

Gifts of stock or insurance
Your financial advisors can help you reap all of the tax benefits from making gifts of appreciated securities and insurance policies. Rather than pay taxes on gains take deductions from accrued gains while helping the Partnership.

Planned gifts
Whether including the Partnership in your estate planning through your will (see correct language below)* or by investing in a gift annuity that pays income for life, planned gifts are excellent vehicles that can help you and the Partnership. Once again, your own financial advisor should be the one to assist you in determining the best option but we can help you explore options that are available.

Give Now

I want to support the Virginia Autism Council

The Virginia Autism Council (VAC), works to advance knowledge and understanding of Autism Spectrum Disorders. The VAC developed the Skill Competencies to effectively support persons with ASD, and advances higher education, training and educational opportunities for those who provide support.

Support the work of the VAC via The Partnership for People with Disabilities at VCU, a 501(c)(3), nonprofit organization today.

At your request, your gifts can be made in honor or in memory of someone you care about.
